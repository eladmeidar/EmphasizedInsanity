I Hate these long, frustrating long messages coming out on me when i look at the log. They are long, and needed a refreshing change for my opinion.here is what i found:There is a method which is responsible for the actual STDOUT printing for the log file, it’s called Logger#format_message. I decided to overrun it in a more pretty way.I created a /lib library called logger_format.rb and required it in environment.rb,class Logger def format_message(level, time, progname, msg) “#{time.to_s(:long)} – #{msg}\n” end end for example.try it out and feel free to modify the output structure at will.
